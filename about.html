We were founded in 1889 in Petersburge, Russia as a mining colony. Founded by Will Ates and Steven Hobs. In 1992 they discovered the gamer culture and industry and moved to China.

In 1932 we at Notendo created our very first game, Call of Duty Modern Warfare 12 for the Atari Jaguar. It was a best seller for 20 years until our next big hit came out in 1926, Spongebob Battle for Bikini Bottom.

Today we make games for the most revelant consoles such as the Family-Tv Game System 750000 in one and the Famicon. Games on the games tab such as Pizzatop Mario and The Legend of Zelda, gives great examples of modern games
